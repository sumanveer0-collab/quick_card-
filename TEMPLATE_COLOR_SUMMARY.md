## 🎯 How to Use

### **Quick Method**
1. Click "Color" tab in sidebar
2. Click any circular color swatch
3. Click "Apply Color Scheme" button
4. Done! All colors update instantly ✅

### **Preview Method**
1. Click "Color" tab
2. Click different swatches to preview
3. See color preview grid update
4. Click "Apply Color Scheme" when ready
5. Done! ✅

## 🎨 What Happens When You Apply

### **Text Elements**
- Dark text → Primary color
- Gold/Yellow text → Accent color
- Other text → White

### **Shape Elements**
- Shapes → Primary/Secondary/Accent
- Borders → Matching colors
- Preserves structure

### **Background**
- Updates to scheme background
- Smooth transition
