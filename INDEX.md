## 🎯 Key Concepts

### **Three-Panel Layout**
- **Left**: Tools and controls (320px)
- **Center**: Canvas preview (flexible)
- **Top**: Control bar (64px)

### **Element System**
- **Text**: Typography controls
- **Image**: Upload & transform
- **Shape**: Geometric elements

### **Layer Management**
- **Z-Index**: Stacking order
- **Controls**: Forward, backward, front, back
- **Lock**: Prevent accidental moves

### **Print Guidelines**
- **Bleed**: 0.125" (12px) - Extra area
- **Trim**: Exact cut line
- **Safety**: 0.1875" (18px) - Keep content inside

### **History System**
- **Undo**: Ctrl+Z
- **Redo**: Ctrl+Y
- **Unlimited**: No limit on history
